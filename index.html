About RJBS JET CLUB

Discover our mission, vision, and what makes our club unique

Inspiring Excellence Since 2015

The RJBS JET CLUB (Junior Engineers and Technologists Club) is a dynamic student organization dedicated to fostering innovation, technical skills, and teamwork among students.

Our motto "To Be The Best" drives everything we do, from competitive robotics to community outreach projects. We believe in pushing boundaries and achieving excellence in all our endeavors.

With over 100 active members and numerous awards, we continue to set the standard for student-led technical clubs in our region.
